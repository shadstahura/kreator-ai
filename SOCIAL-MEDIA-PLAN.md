Show AI doing something impressive. Screen recordings, live demos, results.

## Filming Tips

### Equipment Needed
- Your phone (that's it)
- Natural lighting (face a window)
- Clean background or use a desk setup

### Format
- Vertical video (9:16) for TikTok and Reels
- 30-60 seconds for most videos
- Screen recordings for demos (use QuickTime or Loom)
- Look at the camera, not the screen

### Structure for Every Video
1. **Hook** (first 2 seconds) — the line that stops the scroll
2. **Problem** (5-10 seconds) — make them feel the pain
3. **Solution** (15-30 seconds) — show what AI does
4. **CTA** (5 seconds) — tell them what to do next

## Rules

1. Post every day for 30 days minimum
2. Hook in the first 2 seconds or they scroll
3. Show, don't tell — screen recordings beat talking heads
4. End every video with a clear CTA
5. Reply to every comment in the first hour
6. Don't delete videos that flop — some blow up later
7. Cross-post TikToks to Reels (remove watermark first)
8. Use your real voice — sarcastic, genuine, real
9. Film in batches — knock out 3-4 videos in one session
10. Track what works — double down on your best performers
